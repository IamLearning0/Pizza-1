Our Heritage

From the Streets of Bombay to the Hills of Naples.

Bombay was born from a simple obsession: what happens when you marry the structural integrity of a perfect Neapolitan crust with the complex spice profiles of Indian street food?

We spent three years perfecting our 48-hour fermentation process and sourcing spices directly from small-batch farmers in Maharashtra. The result is a fusion that feels inevitable.
