Our Journey

01. The Obsession Begins

In 2021, our founder returned from Naples with a simple question: "What if we took the soul of Italian pizza-making and married it with the spice heritage of Mumbai?" It wasn't just an idea—it was an obsession. Three years of research followed.

We visited small-batch spice farmers in Maharashtra, studied fermentation techniques in Napoli, and tested over 200 dough recipes. Every detail mattered. From the sourcing of San Marzano tomatoes to the selection of Kashmiri saffron, precision was non-negotiable.

02. The Birth of Bombay

March 2024 changed everything. The first Bandra location opened to a line around the block. Word spread fast—the pizza world had never seen anything like this. Our hand-stretched sourdough meeting the vibrant heat of the subcontinent created a new category of food.

The early days were chaotic, beautiful, and humbling. We learned that perfection wasn't a destination—it was a commitment. Every pizza that left our kitchen carried our obsession with quality.

03. Scaling with Soul

By late 2024, we had proven that fusion didn't mean compromise. Kala Ghoda brought gallery vibes to fine dining. Powai Lake offered sunset views with craft beverages. Each location had its own personality, but the soul remained the same: relentless quality.

We refuse to grow recklessly. Every new menu item, every new location, every new hire is vetted through our obsessive first-principles thinking. Growth for growth's sake is the enemy of soul.

04. The Philosophy

Bombay isn't just about pizza. It's about proving that cultures can speak the same delicious language. It's about honoring both Italian heritage and Indian spice wisdom. It's about saying: "You don't have to choose."

Every sourdough starter we maintain has a name. Every farmer we work with becomes part of our family. Every pizza is an act of love, not just efficiency. We believe great food changes conversations and builds communities.
